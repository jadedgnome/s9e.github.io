When true, some whitespace around this tag will be ignored (not transformed to line breaks.) Up to 2 lines outside of a tag pair and 1 line inside of it: {2 lines}{STARTTAG}{1 line}{CONTENT}{1 line}{ENDTAG}{2 lines}
